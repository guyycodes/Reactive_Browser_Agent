Runbook: Unlock a Locked Account

Category: account_unlock   Urgency: medium   Target app: internal-admin-portal

Preconditions

  • User account must be in status locked. If status is suspended or disabled, escalate to tier-2 — do not unlock.
  • Verify the identity of the requester by matching the ticket email header or by callback to the phone number on file.
  • Confirm the lock is due to failed login attempts (lock reason: too_many_failed_attempts), not a security hold.

Procedure

  1. Navigate to /users.
  2. Search for the affected user.
  3. Open the user detail view. Confirm lock_reason = too_many_failed_attempts.
  4. Click "Unlock Account". The failed-attempt counter resets to zero and status returns to active.
  5. Notify the user in the ticket: they can log in again; recommend a password reset if they cannot remember the password.

Postconditions

User record status is active. Failed-attempt counter is 0. Ticket updated with resolution note.
